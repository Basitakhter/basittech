Recommended Page Section Flow

The following flow organizes sections for optimal user journey and conversion:

  1. Hero Section - Clear value proposition with prominent CTA buttons
  2. About Us Section - Company story and wholesale positioning
  3. Products Showcase - Featured products with CTAs to explore categories
  4. Process Section - How we work, from quote to delivery
  5. Custom Delivery Box - Free consultancy or contact us CTA
  6. Partners & Portfolio - Combined section showing client logos and project examples
  7. Why Choose Us - Competitive advantages with conversion-focused CTAs
  8. Testimonials/Reviews - Social proof from satisfied B2B clients
  9. FAQs Section - Answers to common wholesale sign questions
  10. Footer - Comprehensive navigation and contact information

Key UX/UI Action Items for Developers

Redesign Hero Section

Homepage hero with clear wholesale positioning and strong value proposition above the fold.

Improve Navigation

Better hierarchy and product discoverability with clear segmentation of categories.

Add Trust Indicators

UL certification badges, warranty information, US standards compliance statements.

Optimize Mobile Experience

Mobile-responsive design with larger touch targets and faster loading times.

Implement Conversion CTAs

Sticky CTAs, short quote forms, and streamlined dealer registration funnel.

Improve Performance

Optimize images, enable caching, reduce scripts, improve Core Web Vitals.
